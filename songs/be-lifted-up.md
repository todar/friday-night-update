<strong>(John 12:32; Psalm 24:7)</strong>
Be lifted up, be lifted up
As we bow down
Be lifted up

Let the heavens rejoice
Let the nations be glad
Let the whole earth tremble
For You are God
Come and worship the Lord
In the beauty of holiness
